Merchandise Sales Analysis

Overview

The Merchandise Sales Analysis Dashboard is a dynamic tool designed to provide a comprehensive analysis of sales revenue, quantity sold, and order trends. This Power BI dashboard helps businesses identify top-performing products, customer purchasing patterns, and regional sales performance, enabling data-driven decision-making.

Key Features

  • Sales, Quantity, and Orders Overview:
    • Displays total revenue, total orders, and quantity sold across time periods.
    • Tracks overall trends and compares performance across different months, quarters, and years.
  • Category and Product Analysis:
    • Highlights best-performing product categories and individual products based on sales, quantity sold, and order volume.
    • Identifies low-performing categories to optimize inventory and marketing.
  • Customer Insights:
    • Analyzes customer purchasing behavior, showing the number of orders placed, average order value, and most purchased products.
    • Segments customers based on their contribution to sales and quantity purchased.
  • Geographic Sales Analysis:
    • Provides an interactive view of sales, orders, and quantity sold across regions or countries.
    • Identifies top-performing regions and opportunities for growth in underperforming areas.

Insights Delivered

  1. Sales Trends: Detect seasonal trends, peak sales periods, and revenue fluctuations to optimize marketing strategies.
  2. Product Performance: Understand which products contribute the most to sales, order volume, and quantity sold, helping refine inventory management.
  3. Order Metrics: Gain insights into total orders by different categories.
  4. Regional Insights: Tailor sales strategies by identifying high-potential regions or countries with untapped growth opportunities.
  5. Customer Focus: Enhance customer experience by understanding their preferences and purchase habits

Audience

This dashboard is ideal for sales teams, merchandise managers, and business leaders looking to optimize product performance, customer satisfaction, and regional market strategies.

EV Sales Analysis

Overview

The EV Sales Analysis Dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of the electric vehicle (EV) market in India. Designed for AtliQ Motors, this Power BI dashboard explores sales trends, market penetration, and growth potential. It combines interactive visuals and key performance indicators to deliver actionable insights, enabling informed decision-making for strategic expansion in the EV industry.

Key Features

  • Sales by Maker: Analyzes the performance of key EV manufacturers, showcasing their contribution to the overall market.
  • Regional Insights:Highlights the states with the highest EV penetration rates and tracks growth across regions, offering insights into regional adoption trends.
  • Sales Trends:Examines quarterly and monthly sales trends for two-wheelers and four-wheelers, with projections for future growth.
  • CAGR Analysis: Calculates the Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of top EV manufacturers, identifying market leaders and growth trends.
  • Future Projections: Projects EV sales for 2030 across states, providing insights into potential market size and penetration.
  • Secondary Analysis: Integrates supplementary data to enhance the depth of the analysis, offering a comprehensive view of market dynamics.

Insights Delivered

  1. Identified top-performing states based on EV sales and penetration rates.
  2. Highlighted the fastest-growing EV manufacturers and their contribution to the market.
  3. Provided clear forecasts for future EV sales, aiding long-term strategic planning.
  4. Illustrated the impact of market trends and growth rates across various regions and vehicle categories.

Audience

Business executives, market analysts, and decision-makers seeking insights into the Indian EV market to strategize expansion and identify growth opportunities.

Paralympic Games Analysis

Overview

The dashboard combines interactivity with visually appealing elements to enable an intuitive and insightful user experience.

Key Features

  • Medal Analysis:
    • Highlights the total medal count (Gold, Silver, Bronze) across all years, countries, and sports.
    • Top 5 countries and sports by medal count are displayed using bar charts and tree maps.
    • Historical trends of total medals won over time are illustrated in an engaging line graph.
  • Performance Breakdown:
    • Country Insights:
      Lists the top 10 countries with the most gold medals and shows their overall contributions to the medal tally.
    • Continental Analysis:
      A pie chart represents the distribution of total medals by continent, offering a geographic perspective.
    • Interactive Map:
      Displays total athletes by country using a global map visualization for a worldwide view of participation.
    • Detailed Medal Table:
      Provides a breakdown of medals by country and year for detailed historical reference.

Insights Delivered

  1. Offers a comprehensive overview of Paralympic medal trends, highlighting the dominance of specific countries, athletes, and sports.
  2. Demonstrates how medal distribution has evolved geographically and temporally over decades.
  3. Provides interactive tools to explore medal tallies by year, sport, rank type, and season.
  4. Empowers stakeholders, analysts, and enthusiasts to identify key performance trends in the Paralympic Games.

Audience

Sports analysts, enthusiasts, and organizations interested in understanding the performance trends of Paralympic athletes and nations.

Sales and Finance Analytics

Overview

The Sales and Financial Analytics Report provides a detailed analysis of sales and financial performance for AtliQ Hardware from 2019 to 2021. Using Excel's powerful features like Pivot Tables, Power Query, and Power Pivot, this project delivers actionable insights through a comprehensive report, enabling data-driven decision-making. The report covers critical aspects of sales performance and financial metrics, offering a deep dive into the company's operational and fiscal health.

Key Features

  • Sales Analysis: Evaluated customer net sales performance, market performance vs. targets, and product trends.
  • Financial Analysis: Generated profit and loss statements by fiscal year, month, and division to assess profitability.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Highlighted sales growth, top-performing products, and cost-management strategies.

Insights Delivered

  1. Sales Insights:
    • Customer net sales showed a percentage increase from 2020 to 2021, indicating improved performance.
    • Analyzed market performance vs. targets, highlighting areas exceeding or falling short of expectations.
    • Identified Top 5 and Bottom 5 products based on sales performance.
    • Highlighted new products introduced in 2021 and their impact on revenue.
    • Determined the Top 5 countries driving sales growth.
  2. Financial Insights:
    • Generated Profit & Loss (P&L) statements by fiscal year, showcasing net sales, COGS, gross margin, and gross margin %.
    • Performed a detailed P&L analysis by fiscal month to identify seasonal trends.
    • Analyzed P&L by fiscal year and division, helping assess divisional contributions to profitability.

Audience

  • Business Stakeholders: To monitor overall sales and financial performance across years.
  • Finance Teams: To track profitability trends and optimize cost management strategies.
  • Sales Teams: To evaluate market performance and identify growth opportunities.
